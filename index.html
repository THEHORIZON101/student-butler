FAQ

Built to train better thinking, not louder arguing.

Does Debate Gym pick a side for me?

No. It helps you explain your side, understand the opposing side, and update your position when evidence is stronger.

Is the Pro tier fully working in this static version?

No. This version shows the product experience. Real usage limits, paid plans, school accounts, and report exports need authentication, a database, Stripe, and server-side verification.

Who is this for?

Students, debate clubs, creators, professionals, teachers, and anyone who wants to explain ideas clearly under pressure.
